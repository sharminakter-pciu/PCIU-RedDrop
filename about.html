About PCIU RedDrop

PCIU RedDrop is a student-led blood donation initiative based at Port City International University (PCIU), created to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients in emergency situations.

Our mission is to save lives by making it easy and fast for anyone to request or donate blood through a reliable, transparent, and user-friendly platform.

We believe that no one should suffer due to a lack of access to life-saving blood. Through PCIU RedDrop, we're building a network of compassionate donors who are ready to respond when needed the most.
